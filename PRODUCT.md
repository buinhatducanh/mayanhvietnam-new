# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Professional photographers, content creators, studio owners, and photography enthusiasts across Vietnam. They are technically literate, image-sensitive, and expect a premium digital experience that matches the caliber of the gear they buy. Primary context: researching camera equipment, comparing body+lens combos, buying gear for personal or commercial projects.

## Product Purpose
Máy Ảnh Việt Nam is a camera, lens, drone, and studio equipment retailer operating 4 physical stores across southern Vietnam with an OMO (Online-to-Offline) model. The website exists to showcase premium products in a cinematic showroom, drive online-to-offline purchases, and serve as a trusted technical reference for photographers in Vietnam. Success = high-value orders, strong brand recall, high trust scores, and a photographer community that shops repeatedly.

## Brand Personality
Confident · Technical · Cinematic
Voice: expert but not elitist; precise but warm; Vietnamese pride without jingoism. Tone on product pages is technical and factual; on homepage and banners it's aspirational and cinematic.

## Anti-references
- FPTShop.com.vn: red-heavy, cluttered layout, flash-sale spam, banner overload, no product depth — budget electronics retail, not premium photography.
- Shopee / Lazada: marketplace chaos, no brand identity, pop-ups, fake scarcity, low trust.
- Generic SaaS/cream AI aesthetic: warm beige backgrounds, gradient text, numbered section scaffolding (01/02/03), identical card grids, bounce animations — all tells of AI-generated slop.

## Design Principles
1. **Product is hero** — the UI must recede; the camera is the star of every screen. Generous darkspace, not cluttered chrome.
2. **Technical confidence** — specs, data, and compatibility info earn trust. Show depth rather than fluff. Mono type for prices/specs creates technical credibility.
3. **Cinematic motion** — every animation should feel like it was edited in a grading suite: smooth deceleration, no bounce, no flash. Motion should reveal, not decorate.
4. **Trust signals before push** — warranty info, free shipping thresholds, store locations, and review counts appear before "buy" CTAs. Photographers research extensively before buying expensive gear.
5. **Vietnamese-first UX** — currency in VNĐ, Vietnamese content, local payment methods (MoMo, COD, VNPAY), and Vietnamese copy standards (no machine-translated awkwardness).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.2 Level AA with full keyboard navigation support:
- Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 (body), ≥ 3:1 (large text)
- Keyboard-accessible: all interactive elements focusable and operable, visible focus ring
- Touch targets ≥ 48×48px on mobile
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for all animations
- Screen reader friendly: semantic HTML, aria-labels, correct heading hierarchy
- Alt text on all product images
